About Bayan AR

Bayan AR is the first platform of its kind specialized in teaching the Arabic language. And developing it for non-native speakers, and the Arab heritage and culture, through live broadcasting. In an interesting participatory style between the trainer and the students, away from the boring traditional methods.

Based in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, it includes a number of qualified and competent Saudi trainers who speak their native language Arabic, English and other languages.

Our Vision

Helping everyone around the world to learn the Arabic language, and to gain a closer look at the heritage and The ancient Arab and Islamic culture, without time or geographical restrictions.

To meet the growing demand around the world for learning the Arabic language.

Why Learn Arabic?

The Arabic language represents an ancient Arab and Islamic culture and civilization.

It is a living, contemporary language that represents a wide segment of human societies that are the focus of the world’s attention, as the number of its speakers exceeds 467 million people.

It is the official language of 27 countries and one of the six official languages ​​of the United Nations.

Learning Arabic is witnessing a growing and noticeable demand from non-native speakers for various reasons, including politics, economics, religion, education and tourism.

How to learn with Bayan AR

With Bayan AR you can choose between two distinct learning methods:

The first: A live group class with your teacher for 50 minutes and with 8 students in the group.

This is the best option if you want to build your conversation skills quickly, as 80% of the class time is allocated to dialogue and speaking after new educational content is presented by your teacher.

Then you and your colleagues participate in role-playing, tests and group conversations, which enables you to increase your cognitive abilities and make new friends with people from diverse cultures and nationalities.

All classes are recorded and you can review them if you are unable to attend.

The second: A 50-minute individual lesson with your teacher is more private and more flexible for your schedule and commitments
